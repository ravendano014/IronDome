Iron Dome Enhanced

Defend your cities from the enemy missile rain!

Move the mouse to aim and click to launch a defensive missile.

Each battery has limited missiles, use them strategically!

Press 'S' to toggle sound ON/OFF.

Press 'P' to pause the game.

Press 'I' to activate city immunity for 3 waves (once per level).

Press 'R' to double missile explosion radius for 5 waves (once per level).

Press 'M' to reduce enemy missiles by 4 for 4 waves (once per level).

Press 'E' to activate Iron Beam Laser (Easter Egg)

Click anywhere to start

GAME PAUSED

Press 'P' to resume

Controls:

S - Toggle Sound

I - City Immunity (3 waves)

R - Double Explosion Radius (5 waves)

M - Reduce Enemy Missiles (4 waves)

E - Iron Beam Laser (Easter Egg)
